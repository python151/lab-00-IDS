Exercise 18

We now work on visualising our data.

p1 <- ggplot(data = team_data, 
             mapping = aes(x = github_username, 
                           y = favourite_number,
                           fill = colour), 
             ) +
   geom_col() +
   scale_fill_identity()
p1

The graph displays peoples favorite numbers on a bar graph.
